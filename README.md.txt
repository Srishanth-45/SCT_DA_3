# SCT_DA_3 — HR Employee Attrition Dashboard

## Overview
This Power BI dashboard analyzes employee attrition patterns using the IBM HR Analytics dataset.

## Objective
To identify the major reasons behind employee turnover.

## Key Insights
- Sales department has the highest attrition.
- Employees aged 25–34 show the highest turnover.
- Lower job satisfaction correlates with higher attrition.
- Overtime employees are more likely to leave.
- Lower tenure and lower income employees show higher attrition risk.

## Tools Used
- Power BI
- DAX
- Data Visualization
